About

Guest Speakers

We hold speaker events from USC faculty, researchers, and industry veterans on AI and related topics. Past speakers include Maja Mataric, Steve A. Chien, and Craig Knoblock.

Competitions and Workshops

We offer technical workshops to improve understanding of AI theory. We help people form teams and colloborate on challenging problems through competitions and hackathons,

Industry Networking

We hold information sessions with representatives from organizations like JPL, Northrop Grumman, and Walt Disney Imagineering. Talk to recruiters and gain valuable internship and job opportunities.

Social Events

Join like-minded students on exclusive tours to laboratories and company offices to get an in-depth look into working in AI. Or stop by on social nights to grab a slice of pizza and watch sci-fi movies. Join a tight-knit community full of people who support you.

Perceptron Workshop

Tuesday, February 4th @ 7:30 pm

Perceptrons are the fundamental building blocks of neural network and deep learning algorithms that are taking over the world!
