Hi AAAI,

How is everyone doing? We hope all of you had a great summer. It's been a long time since we sent out our last newsletter. A lot has changed since then. We have a new executive board in place and we have exciting new events lined up for you. We have also moved our website to www.uscaaai.com

We will have our first general meeting on 9/13 where we will introduce all of you to our club so be sure to bring all of your friends along. There will be free food! On top of that, we will end the week with our first movie night on 9/16.

We are one of the few Viterbi student clubs for which students do not need to pay any membership fees and still participate in events. We are also not restricted to graduate or upper class undergrads. This semester we are planning our events targeting everyone from freshman class to seniors and from graduates to PhD. Do spread the word among your peers and ask them to join our newsletter. And as always if you have any thoughts, questions, suggestions or anything to say about activities or newsletter, feel free to visit our website at uscaaai.com or shoot an email to aaai.usc@gmail.com

Nilay Chheda
Professional Outreach Chair
AAAI@USC
